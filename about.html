JIMMY VAN DER HEIJDEN

Hi, I'm Jimmy.

Thank you taking a look on my website.

In my work I always try to stay lighthearted. Playfulness leaves a lot of room for experimentation and out of experimentation come great new things. I search for new ways of interacting with art. These ways can be found in multiple forms of media. Although I'm mostly active in the audio-workfield, I try not to limit myself at one.
